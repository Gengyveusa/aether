## Platform Positioning

Fasolati positions itself as an **AI-driven health operating system** -- not as a medical device, diagnostic tool, or provider of medical advice. Key language from the site:

## Data Protections

### SciAgent Data Handling

The SciAgent AI section ([[07_SciAgent_AI_Architecture]]) explicitly addresses data privacy:

| Protection | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Encrypted at Rest** | AES-256 encryption standard |
| **Never Sold** | Data never sold, rented, or shared with advertisers, insurance companies, or employers |
| **User Control** | Export complete health record anytime. Delete data anytime. Pause collection anytime. |

## SciAgent Behavioral Guarantees

The SciAgent AI system makes three explicit behavioral guarantees:

| Guarantee | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Never Invents** | SciAgent cannot fabricate studies or statistics |
| **Never Overreaches** | If data suggests something outside its scope, it routes to your doctor |
| **Always Auditable** | Every recommendation is logged with its reasoning chain |

### Output Constraints

- SciAgent can **only** recommend interventions from the Fasolati product ecosystem
- Every recommendation comes with an explanation ("why")
- Confidence scoring ensures low-confidence situations are flagged for human clinician review rather than automated

| Category | Description |

### What Fasolati is NOT (Implied)

| Category | Implication |
|----------|------------|
| **Not a medical device** | Fasolati Engine and SciAgent are not FDA-regulated medical devices |
| **Not a diagnostic tool** | Three-vector scoring is health tracking, not clinical diagnosis |

## Ethical Framework

### Transparency Principles

| Principle | Implementation |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Explainable AI** | Every SciAgent recommendation includes its reasoning chain |
| **Confidence scoring** | Users and clinicians see the certainty level of every recommendation |
| **Data sovereignty** | Users own their data; can export or delete at any time |
| **No dark patterns** | Subscription is cancel-anytime; no lock-in |

### Scientific Ethics

| Principle | Implementation |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Evidence-based training** | SciAgent trained on peer-reviewed literature, not internet forums or sponsored content |
| **Clinical guardrails** | Hard-coded safety limits prevent recommendations outside established parameters |
| **Scope limitation** | SciAgent acknowledges uncertainty and routes to human clinicians when appropriate |
| **Constrained outputs** | Cannot recommend non-Fasolati interventions -- prevents off-label or unvalidated suggestions |

## Key Legal Boundaries

| What Fasolati CAN Do | What Fasolati CANNOT Do |
|---------------------|------------------------|
| Track inflammatory biomarkers across three vectors | Diagnose medical conditions |
| Recommend Fasolati products based on user data | Prescribe medications or medical treatments |
| Provide confidence-scored protocol suggestions | Replace clinical evaluation or professional judgment |
| Encrypt and protect user health data (AES-256) | Share identifiable data with third parties |
| Cite peer-reviewed literature for every claim | Fabricate or overstate scientific evidence |
