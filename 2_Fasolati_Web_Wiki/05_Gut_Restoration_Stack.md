The Gut Restoration Stack is Fasolati's product line addressing **Origin Node 2** ([[02_Origin_Node_Framework]]). While the oral products (Lytica, Loria) intercept inflammation at the mouth, the Gut Stack intercepts at the gut -- the body's second controllable upstream source of systemic inflammation.

The Gut Stack is matched to **Vector 2 (Gut Integrity)** in the Fasolati Engine ([[06_Fasolati_Engine_Platform]]):

|-----------|----------|-----------------|
