## The Argument

### 1. Inflammation Is the Primary Mechanism

Chronic inflammatory diseases are not discrete conditions that happen to co-occur. They share a common upstream driver: **sustained, low-grade systemic inflammation** that damages tissues over years before clinical symptoms emerge.

**Diseases sharing this mechanism:**
- Cardiovascular disease (atherosclerosis, ASCVD)
- Cancer (tumor microenvironment inflammation)
- Type 2 diabetes (insulin resistance driven by inflammatory signaling)
- Alzheimer's disease (neuroinflammation)
- Autoimmune disorders (dysregulated immune activation)

### 2. hs-CRP as Early Warning

> "High-sensitivity C-reactive protein is the gold-standard serum marker for systemic inflammation. Over a third of US adults have elevated levels -- most unaware."

| Property | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| **Marker** | hs-CRP (high-sensitivity C-reactive protein) |
| **Function** | Gold-standard serum marker for systemic inflammation |
| **Population prevalence** | 34.6% of US adults have elevated levels |
| **Clinical significance** | hs-CRP > 3.0 mg/L correlates with increased cardiovascular risk |
| **Gap** | Most people with elevated hs-CRP are unaware |

## The Fasolati Position

The Fasolati thesis is not that inflammation is important -- that is established science. The thesis is that **the mouth and gut are controllable origin nodes** where inflammation can be intercepted before it propagates systemically.

### Key Concepts

| Concept | Definition |
|---------|-----------|
| **Origin Node** | A controllable upstream site where systemic inflammatory signaling begins |
| **Interception** | Intervening at the origin node before downstream disease manifests |
| **Node Network** | The connected system of oral, gut, and systemic monitoring + intervention |
| **Operating System** | The Fasolati Engine + product ecosystem functioning as a single coordinated platform |
