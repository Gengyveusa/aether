## Origin Story

> "Fasolati started with a simple observation in an operating room: the mouth and the body have never been treated as one connected system. We're changing that."

### The Surgeon's Observation

> "For nearly two decades, Dr. Thad Connelly has operated at the intersection of the mouth and the body -- as an oral and maxillofacial surgeon performing total joint reconstructions, treating the most severe manifestations of what happens when oral disease goes systemic."

### The Pattern

> "The pattern was always the same: patients arriving with advanced disease that started as inflammation nobody monitored. Periodontitis that seeded systemic infection. Gut dysbiosis that accelerated joint destruction. Biomarkers that were never checked until the damage was done."

### The Insight

> "The insight wasn't that these systems were connected -- the literature has shown that for decades. The insight was that nobody had built the infrastructure to treat them as one system. No product line spanned both origin nodes. No AI correlated the data across domains. No protocol existed that told a patient what to rinse, swallow, and avoid -- today, based on their specific inflammatory profile."

### The Conclusion

> "Fasolati is that infrastructure. Built by a surgeon who got tired of operating on problems that should have been intercepted upstream."

## Timeline

### 2006-2015 -- Clinical Foundation

> "Nearly a decade of oral and maxillofacial surgery across thousands of cases. Pattern recognition: the mouth-body disconnect in clinical practice is systemic, not anecdotal."

### 2016-2019 -- Oral Formulation Development

> "Development of the dual-weight hyaluronic acid platform. First prototypes of what would become Loria -- a chlorhexidine replacement that heals instead of kills. Early clinical validation of the mucosal barrier approach."

### 2020-2022 -- From Product to Platform

> "The realization that a single oral rinse wasn't enough. The mouth-gut-systemic framework takes shape. Partnerships with Magisnat (nutraceuticals), phage therapy researchers, and the Mata lab at Nottingham for enamel regeneration."

### 2023-2024 -- Fasolati Architecture

> "The platform crystallizes: two oral product lines (Lytica, Loria), three gut products (Magisnat, Biolumen, Monch Monch), two distribution tiers (Maxoral Rx, Fasolati OTC), and the SciAgent AI engine tying it all together."

### 2025 -- ELR Breakthrough

> "Mata's ELR enamel regeneration technology published in Nature Communications. A significant advance in biomimetic enamel reconstruction research."

### 2026 -- Node Network Launch

> "Loria goes to market. Fasolati Engine enters beta. DSO partnerships activate. The node network goes from architecture to operating system. The unlit corner starts to glow."
