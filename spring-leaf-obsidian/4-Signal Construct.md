# 4-Signal Minimal Viable Construct

The core product of [[Spring Leaf Bio]] — a precisely formulated combination of four growth factors that recapitulates the [[Neural-First Paradigm]] developmental hierarchy.

## The Four Signals

| # | Factor | Full Name | Role | Phase |
|---|--------|-----------|------|-------|
| 1 | [[NGF]] | Nerve Growth Factor | Neural ingrowth | NERVES |
| 2 | [[PDGF-BB]] | Platelet-Derived Growth Factor BB | Vascular support | VESSELS |
| 3 | [[BMP-7]] | Bone Morphogenetic Protein 7 | PDL differentiation | PDL |
| 4 | [[BDNF]] | Brain-Derived Neurotrophic Factor | Neural survival | Maintenance |

## Delivery System

All four signals are delivered in a [[Hydrogel Technology|dual-crosslinked HA/alginate hydrogel]]:

- **Stiffness:** 300–500 Pa (optimized for periodontal pocket)
- **Format:** Injectable hydrogel
- **Route:** Periodontal pocket injection
- **Crosslinking:** Dual-crosslinked for controlled release

## Why These Four?

This is the **minimal viable** signal set — the fewest factors needed to initiate and sustain the full [[Neural-First Paradigm]] cascade:

- **[[NGF]]** kicks off the neural ingrowth that initiates everything
- **[[PDGF-BB]]** ensures the vascular network forms along neural pathways
- **[[BMP-7]]** drives the terminal differentiation of PDL fibers
- **[[BDNF]]** keeps the newly formed neural tissue alive during maturation

Without any one signal, the cascade stalls or produces incomplete regeneration (see [[Clinical Evidence Gap]]).

## IP Position

The specific 4-signal formulation and its delivery in the [[Hydrogel Technology|hydrogel system]] form Spring Leaf's core IP. This is the basis of the company's **first-mover advantage** in neural-first regeneration — see [[Competitive Landscape]].

## Commercialization Phases

The construct evolves across [[Three-Phase Commercialization]]:

- **Phase 1:** Cell-free (hydrogel + 4 signals only)
- **Phase 2:** Construct + cells (autologous/allogeneic)
- **Phase 3:** Vertically integrated manufacturing

## Regulatory Path

- Phase 1 targets [[Regulatory Strategy|510(k)/De Novo]] with predicates: [[Straumann - Emdogain|Emdogain]], [[BioHorizons - GEM 21S|GEM 21S]]
- Phase 2 requires BLA through CBER
