# Regulatory Strategy

[[Spring Leaf Bio]]'s phased FDA approach for the [[4-Signal Construct]], designed for fastest path to market.

## Phased Regulatory Pathway

### Phase 1: Cell-Free Construct (18–24 months)

| Parameter | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Pathway** | 510(k) or De Novo |
| **Review Division** | CDRH |
| **Timeline** | 18–24 months |
| **Predicates** | [[Straumann - Emdogain|Emdogain (EMD)]], [[BioHorizons - GEM 21S|GEM 21S]] |

**Milestones:**
- **Month 3:** Pre-submission meeting with FDA
- **Month 6:** Biocompatibility testing complete (ISO 10993)
- **Month 12:** Preclinical efficacy data
- **Month 18:** 510(k) submission
- **Month 24:** FDA clearance

**Requirements:**
- ISO 10993 biocompatibility suite
- Shelf-life stability data (24-month target)
- Preclinical efficacy in animal model

### Phase 2: Construct + Cells (36–48 months)

| Parameter | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Pathway** | BLA |
| **Review Division** | CBER |
| **Timeline** | 36–48 months |

**Milestones:**
- **Month 24:** Pre-IND meeting
- **Month 30:** IND submission
- **Month 36:** Phase I clinical trial
- **Month 48:** Phase II clinical trial
- **Month 60:** BLA submission

**Eligible for RMAT designation** (Regenerative Medicine Advanced Therapy).

### Phase 3: Vertical Integration (48–60 months)

| Parameter | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Pathway** | BLA Supplement |
| **Review Division** | CBER |
| **Timeline** | 48–60 months |

**Milestones:**
- **Month 48:** Facility qualification
- **Month 54:** Process validation
- **Month 60:** BLA supplement submission
- **Month 66:** Approval

## Why 510(k) First?

1. **Fastest path** to market — 18–24 months vs. 5+ years for BLA
2. **Revenue generation** — fund Phase 2/3 development
3. **Clinical evidence** — build real-world data for BLA
4. **Manufacturing/QA** — establish [[Quality Systems]] and [[Manufacturing Plan|manufacturing infrastructure]]

## RMAT Strategy

Regenerative Medicine Advanced Therapy designation provides:
- Early FDA interactions
- Rolling BLA submission
- Priority review
- Accelerated approval pathway

## FDA Engagement Timeline

```
Pre-Sub → Q-Sub → 510(k) Review (90-day) → Clearance
```
