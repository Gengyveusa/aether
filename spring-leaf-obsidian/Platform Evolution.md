# Platform Evolution — Gengyve to Spring Leaf

The evolution from **Gengyve / Adirondack Intelligence** to [[Spring Leaf Bio]] — same science, same team, same vision, optimized for clinical translation.

## The Transition

| Aspect | Why the Change |
|--------|---------------|
| **Same science** | [[Neural-First Paradigm]] and [[4-Signal Construct]] unchanged |
| **Same team** | Founder & CEO continues to lead (see [[Team and Hiring]]) |
| **Same vision** | High-fidelity periodontal regeneration |
| **Optimized for clinical translation** | Regulatory-ready naming and positioning |
| **Built for regulatory success** | Clear [[Regulatory Strategy|FDA pathway]] |
| **Designed for scalable manufacturing** | [[Manufacturing Plan|Three-phase manufacturing]] strategy |

## Why Rebrand?

The move from Gengyve/Adirondack Intelligence to Spring Leaf Bio reflects maturation from research concept to clinical-stage company:

1. **Clinical translation focus** — name and brand optimized for healthcare market
2. **Regulatory positioning** — clean corporate identity for FDA interactions
3. **Investor clarity** — single brand, clear mission
4. **Scalable identity** — designed to grow from seed to commercial stage
