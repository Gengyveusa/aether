# Market Opportunity

The addressable market for [[Spring Leaf Bio]]'s [[4-Signal Construct]] in periodontal regeneration.

## Market Sizing

| Segment | Value |
|---------|-------|
| **Global periodontal regeneration** | $4.2B |
| **CAGR** | 8.2% |
| **US addressable** | $1.1B |
| **DSO beachhead** | $340M |

## Why This Market?

The [[Clinical Evidence Gap]] creates massive unmet need:

- **87%** incomplete regeneration with current products
- **30–50%** recurrent disease within 5 years
- Current products ([[Straumann - Emdogain|Emdogain]], [[Geistlich - Bio-Oss|Bio-Oss]], [[BioHorizons - GEM 21S|GEM 21S]]) produce structure without function
- No competitor addresses the neural component — see [[Competitive Landscape]]

## Beachhead: DSOs

The [[DSO Go-to-Market Strategy]] targets the $340M DSO segment first because:

- Centralized purchasing decisions
- Standardized clinical protocols
- Data-driven outcome tracking
- Volume pricing models
- Faster adoption cycles
- Reference site potential

- Aging population with increasing periodontal disease prevalence
- Rising awareness of oral-systemic health connections
- DSO consolidation creating larger purchasing entities
- Shift toward regenerative (vs. resective) treatment paradigms
- [[Neural-First Paradigm]] as clinically differentiable outcome
