# NGF — Nerve Growth Factor

Signal #1 in the [[4-Signal Construct]]. The primary initiator of the [[Neural-First Paradigm]] cascade.

## Role in the Construct

- **Function:** Initiates neural ingrowth into the regenerating periodontal tissue
- **Phase:** NERVES (first phase of the cascade)
- **Mechanism:** Promotes axonal growth, guides neural pathways into the defect site
- **Criticality:** Without NGF, the neural-first cascade cannot begin

## Biological Function

NGF is a neurotrophic factor essential for:
- Growth and maintenance of peripheral sensory neurons
- Axonal guidance and branching
- Neural survival during development
- Pain signaling and proprioception

In periodontal regeneration, NGF-driven neural ingrowth is the **initiating event** that triggers subsequent vascular patterning ([[PDGF-BB]]) and PDL differentiation ([[BMP-7]]).

## Cascade Position

```
[NGF] → Neural ingrowth
   ↓
[[PDGF-BB]] → Vascular network
   ↓
[[BMP-7]] → PDL differentiation
   ↓
[[BDNF]] → Neural maintenance
```
