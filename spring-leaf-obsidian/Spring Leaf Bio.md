# Spring Leaf Bio

> **High Fidelity Periodontal Regeneration**
> *"The first regenerative construct that recapitulates the neural-first developmental hierarchy"*

Spring Leaf Bio is a periodontal regeneration biotech company building on the foundational work of [[Platform Evolution|Gengyve / Adirondack Intelligence]]. The company is pioneering a [[Neural-First Paradigm]] approach to periodontal tissue regeneration using a proprietary [[4-Signal Construct]] delivered via an injectable [[Hydrogel Technology|dual-crosslinked hydrogel]].

---

## Core Science

- **Paradigm:** [[Neural-First Paradigm]] — NERVES → VESSELS → PDL
- **Product:** [[4-Signal Construct]] (NGF, PDGF-BB, BMP-7, BDNF)
- **Delivery:** [[Hydrogel Technology]] — HA/alginate, 300–500 Pa
- **Problem:** [[Clinical Evidence Gap]] — current therapies produce structure without function
