# Supply Chain

[[Spring Leaf Bio]]'s raw material sourcing strategy for the [[4-Signal Construct]] and [[Hydrogel Technology]].

## Key Components

| Component | Source | Notes |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| **Hyaluronic acid (HA)** | Fermentation-derived | Domestic, consistent quality |
| **Alginate** | Seaweed-derived | Natural polymer, well-characterized |
| **Growth factors** | Recombinant (Phase 1–2) / In-house (Phase 3) | [[NGF]], [[PDGF-BB]], [[BMP-7]], [[BDNF]] |
| **Delivery system** | Pre-filled syringes | Medical-grade, sterile packaging |

## Growth Factor Sourcing Evolution

| Phase | Source | Cost Impact |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| Phase 1 | Commercial recombinant suppliers | Highest cost component |
| Phase 2 | Commercial recombinant suppliers | Same, cells add cost |
| Phase 3 | **In-house recombinant production** | Major COGS reduction |

In-house growth factor production is the key driver of the [[Manufacturing Plan|COGS reduction from $650 to $420]] in Phase 3.

## Domestic Sourcing Strategy

[[Spring Leaf Bio]] prioritizes US-based sourcing:

| Region | Function |
|--------|----------|
| **NE corridor** | Biomanufacturing operations (proximity to CMOs, talent) |
| **Research Triangle** | R&D facilities (academic partnerships, talent) |
| **California** | Component sourcing (biotech supplier ecosystem) |

## Supply Chain Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Growth factor supply disruption | Dual-source qualification, safety stock |
| HA quality variability | Fermentation-derived (more consistent than animal-derived) |
| Alginate batch variation | Incoming QC per [[Quality Systems]] |
| Syringe supply | Standard medical component, multiple suppliers |

## Quality Requirements

All incoming materials tested per [[Quality Systems]]:
- Certificate of Analysis (CoA) for each lot
- Incoming QC inspection
- Supplier qualification and audit program
- Full traceability (lot-to-lot)

## Cost Structure Impact

Supply chain optimization drives the [[Manufacturing Plan|COGS progression]]:
- **Phase 1:** $180/unit (CMO markups, commercial GFs)
- **Phase 2:** $140/unit (-22%, volume pricing, partial in-house)
- **Phase 3:** $85/unit (-53%, full vertical integration)
