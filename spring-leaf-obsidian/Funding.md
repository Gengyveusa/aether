# Funding — The Ask

[[Spring Leaf Bio]] is raising a **$3.5M seed round** to fund 18 months of operations through [[Regulatory Strategy|510(k) submission]].

## Use of Funds

| Category | Allocation | Amount |
|----------|-----------|--------|
| **R&D / Product Development** | 45% | $1,575,000 |
| **Regulatory** | 25% | $875,000 |
| **Manufacturing Scale-up** | 20% | $700,000 |
| **Operations / G&A** | 10% | $350,000 |
| **Total** | 100% | **$3,500,000** |

### R&D / Product Development ($1.575M)
- [[4-Signal Construct]] optimization
- [[Hydrogel Technology]] refinement
- Preclinical efficacy studies
- [[Neural-First Paradigm]] validation

### Regulatory ($875K)
- [[Regulatory Strategy|Pre-submission meeting]] (Month 3)

- Preclinical study design and execution
- 510(k)/De Novo submission preparation

### Manufacturing Scale-up ($700K)
- [[Manufacturing Plan|CMO partnership]] setup ($200–300K)
- Process development
- [[Quality Systems]] establishment
- Initial batch production

### Operations / G&A ($350K)
- [[Team and Hiring|Key hires]] (VP R&D, Regulatory Lead)
- Facilities
- Legal / IP
- General operations
