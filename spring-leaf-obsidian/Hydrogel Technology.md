# Hydrogel Technology

The delivery platform for [[Spring Leaf Bio]]'s [[4-Signal Construct]] — a dual-crosslinked hyaluronic acid/alginate hydrogel optimized for periodontal pocket injection.

## Specifications

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Composition** | Hyaluronic acid + alginate |
| **Crosslinking** | Dual-crosslinked |
| **Stiffness** | 300–500 Pa |
| **Format** | Injectable |
| **Delivery route** | Periodontal pocket injection |
| **Packaging** | Pre-filled syringes |
| **Shelf life** | 24 months at 2–8°C |

## Why HA/Alginate?

- **Hyaluronic acid (HA):** Native to periodontal tissues, biocompatible, supports cell migration
- **Alginate:** Enables ionic crosslinking, tunable degradation, well-characterized safety profile
- **Dual crosslinking:** Provides both rapid gelation (in situ) and sustained mechanical integrity

## Stiffness Optimization (300–500 Pa)

The 300–500 Pa range is specifically tuned for the periodontal pocket environment:

- Soft enough to inject through a syringe
- Firm enough to maintain position in the pocket
- Matched to native periodontal tissue modulus
- Supports the [[Neural-First Paradigm]] — neural ingrowth requires specific mechanical cues
