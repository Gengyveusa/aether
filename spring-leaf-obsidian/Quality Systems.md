# Quality Systems

[[Spring Leaf Bio]]'s quality management framework supporting [[Regulatory Strategy|FDA compliance]] and [[Manufacturing Plan|manufacturing operations]].

## Regulatory Standards

| Standard | Scope | Phase |
|----------|-------|-------|
| **21 CFR 820** | Quality System Regulation (QSR) | All phases |
| **ISO 13485** | Medical device QMS | All phases |
| **cGMP for biologics** | Current Good Manufacturing Practice | Phase 2+ |

## Testing Protocol

### Release Testing (Every Batch)

| Test | Method | Specification |
|------|--------|---------------|
| **Sterility** | USP <71> | No growth |
| **Endotoxin** | USP <85> (LAL) | <5 EU/mL |
| **GF potency** | In-house bioassay | Each of [[NGF]], [[PDGF-BB]], [[BMP-7]], [[BDNF]] within spec |
| **Rheology** | Oscillatory rheometry | 300–500 Pa ([[Hydrogel Technology]]) |

### Stability Testing

- **Target shelf life:** 24 months
- **Storage condition:** 2–8°C
- **Monitoring:** Potency, sterility, rheology over time
- Required for [[Regulatory Strategy|510(k) submission]]

### Biocompatibility (ISO 10993)

Required by Month 6 per [[Funding|18-month milestones]]:
- Cytotoxicity
- Sensitization
- Irritation
- Systemic toxicity
- Hemocompatibility

## Quality by Phase

### Phase 1 (CMO)
- CMO must be ISO 13485 certified
- Spring Leaf provides specifications and incoming QC
- Batch release by Spring Leaf quality team
- See [[Manufacturing Plan|Phase 1 manufacturing]]

### Phase 2 (Hybrid)
- In-house QC laboratory established
- CMO handles bulk manufacturing
- Spring Leaf performs all release testing
- cGMP compliance for cell-based components

### Phase 3 (In-House)
- Full in-house QMS
- cGMP biologics facility
- Complete control over process and quality
- Facility qualification required (Month 48)

## CMC Lead

The [[Team and Hiring|CMC Lead]] (hire by Month 6) owns quality system establishment, CMO oversight, and regulatory compliance documentation.
