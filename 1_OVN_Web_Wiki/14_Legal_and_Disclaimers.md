# Legal & Disclaimers

> Applies to: all pages (footer and inline caveats throughout the platform)

---

## Overview

OVN Nexus maintains strict epistemic guardrails throughout all content. Every page carries disclaimer language, and the platform's evidence-tier system (see [[01_Home_OVN_Axis_and_Platform#Evidence Tiers (Epistemic Honesty)]]) ensures that claims are never presented as stronger than the supporting evidence warrants.

---

## Core Disclaimers

### 1. Professional-Education Content Only

All content on OVN Nexus — including education modules, research presentations, biomarker data, and risk predictions — is intended **exclusively for professional education and research purposes**. It is designed for licensed clinicians, researchers, and academics operating within their scope of practice.

### 2. Not Medical Advice

Nothing on the platform constitutes medical advice, clinical guidance, or a recommendation for any specific treatment protocol. Clinicians must exercise independent professional judgment in all patient care decisions. The platform provides information to inform clinical reasoning, not to replace it.

### 3. Causal-Inference Guardrails

This is the most critical legal and scientific distinction on the platform:

| What You CAN Say | What You CANNOT Say |
|------------------|---------------------|
| Periodontitis is **associated with** increased cardiovascular risk | Periodontitis **causes** heart disease |
| Periodontal treatment **improves surrogate markers** (hsCRP, IL-6) | Periodontal treatment **prevents** heart attacks |
| OMVs **can cross barriers and trigger inflammation** in lab models | OMVs **are proven to cause** Alzheimer's disease |
| The OVN Axis is a **working scientific framework** | The OVN Axis is **established medical fact** |
| Biomarker patterns **suggest** elevated risk | Biomarker results **diagnose** systemic disease |

### 4. Users Must Not Overclaim

Platform members and users must not:

- Present periodontal disease as a **proven causal driver** of ASCVD, cancer, or Alzheimer's
- Use OVN Nexus content to make **diagnostic claims** to patients
- Represent AI risk prediction scores as **clinical diagnoses**
- Extrapolate **in vitro** or **animal model** findings directly to human clinical outcomes
- Present the 5-step cascade as **validated** rather than **hypothesized**

### 5. Research Data Limitations

- All patient data is de-identified and used for research purposes only
- Biomarker tracking provides population-level trends, not individual clinical guidance
- AI/ML risk predictions are research tools, not FDA-approved diagnostics
- Clinical trial information is for research coordination, not patient self-enrollment

## Evidence Tier Compliance

Every claim on the platform must be tagged with its appropriate evidence tier:

| Tier | Label | Meaning | Language Required |
|------|-------|---------|-------------------|
| 1 | **Established** | Supported by meta-analyses and large epidemiological studies | "Evidence shows…", "Meta-analyses confirm…" |
| 2 | **Supported** | Demonstrated in laboratory/animal models with biological plausibility | "Laboratory evidence suggests…", "In vitro studies demonstrate…" |
| 3 | **Hypothesis Under Test** | Theoretically grounded but awaiting clinical validation | "It is hypothesized that…", "This model proposes…", "Currently under investigation…" |

Content that mixes tiers without clear labeling violates platform standards.
