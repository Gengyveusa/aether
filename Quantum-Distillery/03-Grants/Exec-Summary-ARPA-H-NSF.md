# Executive Summary — Quantum Biomarkers for Predictive Health

> *One-pager for ARPA-H DELPHI and NSF program managers*

---

## Problem

Predictive medicine has a **quantum blind spot**. Current diagnostics detect disease after molecular cascades produce macroscopic pathology — missing a 5-15 year window where quantum-level dysregulation precedes clinical detection. No existing platform simultaneously reads the quantum mechanical fingerprints that distinguish cancer from aging at the metabolic level.

---

## Insight: Opposing Quantum Dysregulations

Four quantum biological architectures are **oppositely dysregulated** in cancer vs. aging:

| Architecture | Cancer (Amplified) | Aging (Decohered) |
|---|---|---|
| FMO Coherence | Enhanced coupling, ↑ATP | Disrupted coupling, ↓efficiency |
| Enzyme Tunneling | ↑KIE, enhanced catalysis | ↓KIE, slower metabolism |
| Mito ETC Transfer | Supercomplex stabilization | Supercomplex disassembly |
| Cryptochrome Spin | Extended radical pair | Trp degradation, ↓coherence |

---

## Solution: Probius QES + Quantum Protection Index

The **Probius Quantum Electrochemical Sensor (QES)** measures four metabolites from a single 50 μL finger-stick:

**NADH · FAD · ATP · GSH**

These metabolites are the *electrochemical fingerprints* of all four quantum architectures. The **Quantum Protection Index (QPI)** integrates them:

```
QPI = w1·f(NADH) + w2·f(FAD) + w3·f(ATP) + w4·f(GSH)
QPI > 0 → Cancer    |    QPI < 0 → Aging    |    QPI ≈ 0 → Healthy
```
