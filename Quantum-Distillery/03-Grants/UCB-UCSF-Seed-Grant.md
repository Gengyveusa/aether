# UCB-UCSF Seed Grant — Quantum Biomarker Pilot

> **$50K seed collaboration with Lanzara Lab (UCB Physics)**
> Paired QES + ultrafast spectroscopy validation study

---

## Aims

### Aim 1: Establish QES-Spectroscopy Correlation
Measure NADH, FAD, ATP, and GSH via Probius QES in 50 paired blood samples (25 cancer, 25 age-matched controls) and correlate with Lanzara mid-IR pump-probe coherence lifetime measurements on matched mitochondrial isolates.

### Aim 2: Validate Opposing Signatures in Paired Cohort
Demonstrate statistically significant opposing patterns in cancer vs. aging-matched samples across all four analytes. Compute preliminary QPI scores and assess separation power (target AUC > 0.75).

### Aim 3: Generate Preliminary Data for ARPA-H DELPHI
Package QES + spectroscopy paired dataset as the evidence base for the DELPHI Solution Summary submission. Establish sample collection SOPs and data integration pipeline for the full-scale study.
